How it works

Validation at every step

SA-Core intercepts every AI interaction through an 8-phase execution loop. Each phase has a job. Nothing is skipped.

1

Input Validation

Every request is parsed and validated before it reaches your AI. Malformed, unsafe, or out-of-scope commands are rejected at the gate.

2

Anomaly Detection

Eight heuristic checks run against every AI response. Semantic drift, hallucination markers, and inconsistency patterns are flagged automatically.

3

Explanation-Driven Repair

When a response is flagged, SA-Core doesn't just reject it — it reasons about what went wrong and attempts a corrected reply before falling back.

4

Reasoning Integrity

SA-Core runs its own reasoning chain before every AI call. The AI's output is validated against SA-Core's independent reasoning — not just rules.

5

Audit Trail

Every decision is logged in append-only JSONL. Security events, flagged responses, and repairs are all traceable and inspectable.

6

Agent Control

Only authorised agents may connect. SA-Core enforces a strict allowlist — no rogue agents, no unauthorised AI providers, no exceptions.
